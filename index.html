01 – About
Who I Am

"I care about people, and I'm building the skills to serve them at the highest level."

I'm Aaron Strasler, a future healthcare professional from Garland, Texas, who has spent over a decade learning what it means to work in high-stakes clinical environments with precision, patience, and care.

My career began in direct clinical settings at Octapharma and Grifols, where I performed venipuncture and phlebotomy, managed plasma processing workflows, ensured FDA regulatory compliance, and trained incoming staff. These experiences gave me a deep respect for accuracy, protocol, and the trust patients place in healthcare workers.

Now I'm channeling that foundation into something I'm genuinely excited about: magnetic resonance imaging. I'm currently completing prerequisites for the MRI Technologist A.A.S. program at Dallas College Brookhaven (target entry Spring 2027), and every step is a deliberate move toward a career I've chosen with intention.
